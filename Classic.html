I love some Late Romanticism symphonic and Piano works. Typcially, Anton Bruckner and Gustav Mahler are my favorite composers. Though they have different expression in their music, they both show their philosophy about the life. Mahler's symphony No.7 is the best in the history, and I think Bruckner's No.8 holds the second place. An Italian conductor, Claudio Abbado is my favorite, his interpretation about Mahler is terrific. For now, besides Bernard Haitink, Simon Rattle would be the best in the world, let's see his return to London in next few years.


I also hold several CD recordings with a wide range including the works of Bach, Haydn, Beethoven, Mozart, Schubert, Berlioz, Chopin, Brahms, Bruckner, Mahler, Smatana, Dvorak, Rossini, Verdi, Tchaikovsky, Rachmaninov, Prokovief and Shostakovich. Addabdo and DG recorded Schubert's symphony No.9 "GREAT" several times, I like all of them and I think this is the best work of Schubert.


Talking about some piano concerto, Beethoven's No.4 and No.5 are really impressive, and Chopin's two concerto, Brahms's two, Prokovief's No.2 and No.3. I don't need to say each of them one by one, they are the pieces I usually listen when I stay alone. I did not touch so much of other aspects because I believe piano concerto is a kind of powerful tool to air the inner feelings. I love the Austrian pianist Alfred Brendel, his version of Beethoven, Schubert and Brahms is remarkable.

Today, there are some famous Chinese pianist such as Yundi Li, Lang Lang, and Yujia Wang, they gained their reputation from different way but they are all good. Comparing to other historical legends like Rubinstein and Holowitz, they still have a long period to catch up. The most hard thing for them is interpretation since the culture difference between China and Europe, however, today's social work and plenty of resources are useful enough for them to eliminate the culture difference. I like Yujia better not because of her debut in Lucerne Festival under Abbado, her performance is a combination of inner and outer and still can see a inclination of skills and philosophy.
